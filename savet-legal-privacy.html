SaveT Privacy Policy

Draft policy page, subject to legal review.

This policy explains how SaveT handles personal data and saved content.

Scope

This policy applies to SaveT product usage, including mobile app and related backend services.

Data categories

  • Account and authentication data
  • User-generated saved items (links, notes, tags, metadata)
  • Technical and diagnostic logs for reliability and security

How SaveT uses data

  • Provide core bookmarking and organization functionality
  • Sync and secure user data across sessions/devices
  • Improve features, reliability, and product quality

AI and enrichment features

When enabled, enrichment features may process content metadata through integrated providers. SaveT is designed with fail-safe behavior so core usage remains available if enrichment fails.

Data sharing and processors

Data may be processed by infrastructure and service providers required for product operation (for example authentication, hosting, and databases). SaveT does not sell user data.

Retention and deletion

SaveT may use soft-delete workflows and retention windows to prevent accidental loss. Final deletion behavior will be documented in the production policy version.

Contact

For SaveT privacy requests, contact support@mgonnacrusht.co.uk.
