Last updated: May 5, 2026

This policy explains how SaveT handles personal data and saved content.

Scope

This policy applies to the SaveT mobile app and related backend services.

Data We Collect

  • Account data, including email address handled through Firebase Authentication.
  • User-generated content, including saved links, notes, tags, and related metadata.
  • Technical logs used for reliability, security, diagnostics, and service operation.

How SaveT uses data

  • Provide core bookmarking and organization functionality
  • Sync and secure user data across sessions/devices
  • Improve features, reliability, and product quality

Third Party Services

SaveT may use the following third party services to provide app functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication and Firestore by Google: Google Privacy Policy
  • Gemini API by Google for AI enrichment with user consent: Google Privacy Policy
  • Grok API by xAI for AI enrichment with user consent: xAI Privacy Policy
  • Iframely for URL metadata and embeds: Iframely Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API for map data: Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube Data API for video metadata: Google Privacy Policy

AI Enrichment

  • AI enrichment is active only with user consent.
  • URL data and content metadata may be sent to third party AI servers for enrichment.
  • Connection data, including IP address, may be processed by those third party services.
  • You can disable AI enrichment at any time from app settings.

Data sharing and processors

Data may be processed by infrastructure and service providers required for product operation (for example authentication, hosting, and databases). SaveT does not sell user data.

User Rights (GDPR)

  • You have the right to access your personal data.
  • You have the right to delete your personal data.
  • You have the right to object to processing where applicable.
  • You can delete your account and all related data from inside the app.
  • To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at legal@mgonnacrusht.co.uk with your registered email address. We will process your request within 30 days.

Data Retention

  • When you delete your account, all related Firestore data is deleted.
  • Local cache is cleared when the app is removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

SaveT is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended for use by children under 13, consistent with COPPA requirements.

International Data Transfer

Your data may be processed on servers based in the United States or other countries through providers such as Google and xAI.

Contact

For SaveT privacy requests, contact legal@mgonnacrusht.co.uk.
