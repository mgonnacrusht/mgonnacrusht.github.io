# One inbox for everything
SaveT is a bookmarking and organization app by MgonnacrushT that keeps links, places, media, and ideas in one searchable workspace.

Built for people who collect too much online, SaveT replaces scattered tabs, screenshots, notes, and app-specific lists with a single, simple system.

- Capture fast with a quick-save flow.
- Organize with nested lists and tags.
- Rediscover with search and breadcrumb context.
- Improve quality with AI-supported enrichment.
